The most popular destinations

Nice, France

Posted by Robert Alex Ramascanu on August 24, 2020

Nice, the great urban center of the French Riviera, has always benefited from its exceptional location, between the sea and the amphitheater of the hills. With about 340,000 inhabitants it is the fifth largest city in France and capital of the Alpes-Maritimes department, in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region.

London, UK

Posted by Robert Alex Ramascanu on September 18, 2019

London is the capital of the United Kindom of Great Britain and Nothern Ireland. It is a cosmopolitan city. It was founded by the Romans, who gave it the name of Londinium. It is divided into two parts: West End (the rich part) and Est End (the poor part).

Salerno, Italy

Posted by Robert Alex Ramascanu on July 24, 2019

Salerno is a fascinating synthesis of what the Mediterranean can offer to all those who want to know it more closely. The city has almost 140,000 inhabitants and covers an area of ​​5,975.32 hectares. ... And it is in the historic center of the city that the Salerno nightlife takes place.

Verona, Italy

Posted by Robert Alex Ramascanu on March 8, 2019

Verona, the provincial capital of Veneto, is located in the plain at the mouth of the Adige valley, at the foot of the Lessini mountains. An ancient Paleoveneto settlement, the city became rich and powerful with the Romans, who appreciated its natural strategic position, a crossing point towards the north of Europe.
